About The BMW Brand

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke) is a leading German company in the luxury car and motorcycle industry. Founded in 1916 and based in Munich, BMW is famous for its series of high-performance luxury cars with distinctive designs, in addition to the M division that produces high-performance vehicles. BMW offers a unique driving experience that combines luxury, performance, and advanced technology.
