About Mercedes-Benz

Mercedes-Benz is a leading German luxury automobile manufacturer. Founded in 1926 and headquartered in Stuttgart, Mercedes-Benz is renowned for its luxury car series with distinctive design and advanced technology, in addition to its AMG division which produces high-performance vehicles. Mercedes-Benz offers a unique driving experience that combines luxury, performance, and technological innovation, with a focus on safety, comfort, and sustainability.
