About the Audi Brand

Audi is a German luxury car manufacturer founded in 1909 and headquartered in Ingolstadt. Audi is known for its innovative design, high build quality, and the famous **Quattro** all-wheel-drive technology which provides superior performance and stability. Audi offers a wide range of vehicles that combine elegance, sportiness, and advanced technology, and is a part of the Volkswagen Group.
